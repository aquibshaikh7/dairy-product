Ensuring Every Drop is Pure, Safe & Nutritious

Our Milk Quality Testing Process

At our dairy, we follow a rigorous multi-step testing process to ensure the milk
you receive is of the highest quality—free from antibiotics, hormones, adulterants, and contamination.

1. On-Farm Milk Collection

  • Temperature check (to ensure freshness)
  • Lactometer test (to check density and detect dilution)
  • Organoleptic check (smell, taste, color)
  • Adulteration tests: water, starch, urea, detergents

2. Milk Arrival at Processing Unit

  • SNF & Fat Content Analysis
  • Antibiotic Residue Test
  • Hormone Screening

3. Chilling and Storage

Milk is immediately chilled to below 4°C to preserve freshness and prevent bacterial growth. Storage is done in food-grade stainless steel tanks under hygienic conditions.

4. Final Quality Check Before Dispatch

Before packaging and dispatch, the milk undergoes a final quality check to confirm:

  • Purity
  • Shelf-life readiness
  • Absence of contamination
  • Compliance with FSSAI standards

Only after passing all tests is the milk approved for delivery to customers.

Testing Technology & Standards

  • We use FSSAI-certified labs and food-grade testing kits
  • Follow ISO standards for dairy hygiene
  • Regular audits & third-party lab validations ensure transparency
